Here, we the students of Class 11th A are presenting Our Model - Laser parallax measuring device, Which is not invented yet anywhere, Our device is capable of measuring distances from 1 meter to 1920 meters, with ACCURACY and PRECISION.
Our device works on the principle of Parallax method which is used to measure long distances, Here with our device we can measure small distances without using traditional measuring Methods, Simply here we are eliminating the concept of measuringtapes and scales. Our device is much more accurate, precise and fast since is heavily based on mathematical calculations. Our instrument is complemented by a computer application which works on an algorith back of the screen, leaving no chance of mistake in Measurement.
